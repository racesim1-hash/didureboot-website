Frequently asked questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Do you work on Macs as well as PCs? ▼

Yes! We support both Windows PCs and Apple Mac computers. Whether it's hardware repair, software issues, or setup, we've got you covered on both platforms.

How fast can you come out? ▼

We offer same-day service for most issues. Our average response time is 2 hours. For urgent business issues, we prioritize getting to you as fast as possible.

Do you offer remote support? ▼

Absolutely! Many issues can be resolved remotely without us ever coming to your home or office. Remote support is available at the same hourly rate and is often faster.

What if you can't fix my issue? ▼

Simple — if we can't fix it, you don't pay. We believe in honest, results-based service. We'll always tell you upfront if something is beyond repair.

What areas do you serve? ▼

We serve all of South Orange County including Ladera Ranch, Rancho Santa Margarita, Mission Viejo, Lake Forest, Aliso Viejo, San Clemente, Dana Point, and surrounding areas.

Do you help with smart home and security camera setup? ▼

Yes! Smart home setup is one of our specialties. We install and configure security cameras, smart locks, doorbells, Nest/Ecobee thermostats, and full home automation systems.

How do I pay? ▼

We accept cash, Venmo, Zelle, and all major credit cards. Payment is due upon completion of the work. For larger projects we may require a deposit upfront.
